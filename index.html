About

Haozhe Tian holds a MSc (distinction) in Communications and Signal Processing from Imperial College London, UK. Haozhe received his BEng in Automation, with a specialization in pattern recognition and control, from Beihang University, China. Beihang University is a top university in China, requiring top 0.2% grade in the national college entrance exam.

Haozhe's research interests are:

  • biomedical signal processing (EEG, ECG, PPG)
  • graph neural network
  • representation learning
  • self-supervised learning
