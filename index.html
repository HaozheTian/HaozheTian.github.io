After receiving a scam call, I realized the insecurity of PDF sharing/cloud backup. I came up with two pieces of softwares to ensure the safe online sharing of PDF files.

  • The PDFWatermarker automatically adds customized watermarks to PDF files.
  • The PDFEncryptor encrypts/decrypts PDF files using AES. This software targets online sharing. Since the encrypted PDF cannot be viewed without the key, the user does not need to worry about malicious service providers.
